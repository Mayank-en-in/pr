Project Details

Crafted with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this expansive web application employs dropdown menus, a sophisticated search bar, strategically placed *br* tags, and
hyperlinks to dynamically showcase the rich bio-data of students. Its design ensures responsiveness across devices, providing an immersive user experience. The
user-friendly interface seamlessly integrates interactive elements, allowing effortless exploration of detailed student profiles. Emphasizing accessibility and
engaging visuals, this project creates a liberal and captivating platform for users to navigate and discover comprehensive student information. The entire project,
driven by front-end technologies, serves as a testament to the seamless fusion of design and functionality.
